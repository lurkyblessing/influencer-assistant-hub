Lumina Hub

Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: June 6, 2026

Lumina Hub values your control over your data. In compliance with Facebook (Meta), Google, and TikTok developer requirements, we provide this guide and self-service option to purge your workspace information.

How We Process Your Data

Lumina Hub stores all workspace records, connected account settings, planning posts, and tokens directly inside your local web browser database (LocalStorage). We do not collect, transmit, or retain this data on external servers.

Option 1: Self-Service Deletion (Immediate Purge)

Clicking the button below will wipe all local data records associated with Lumina Hub from your browser storage, including settings, active posts, linked channels, and access tokens.

Clear Local Workspace Cache

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Option 2: Revoking Third-Party App Permissions

If you linked TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube accounts and wish to revoke API access credentials on the platform's side:

  • Instagram/Facebook: Go to your Facebook Account Settings > Apps and Websites > Find **Lumina Hub** > Click **Remove**.
  • Google (YouTube): Access Google Account Settings > Security > Third-party apps with account access > Find **Lumina Hub** > Click **Remove Access**.
  • TikTok: Go to TikTok Settings > Security and Login > Manage app access > Find **Lumina Hub** > Click **Remove Access**.
